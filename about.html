INTRODUCTION
The Missing Child has been the most anticiapted horror series since the invention of the radio in the 1920s. In each episode, a story of a missing child is narrated through an auditory experience. The series has a total of 6 parts with each part having its own unique theme. Despite the modern inventions in visual technology, the missing child has dominated the horror industry through audiory experience alone. The haunted thriller is tuned in every week by millions of viewers around the globe.
DID YOU KNOW?
The Missing Child was the first horror thriller to be aired on a radio and the first horror series to be premiered globally. The show has 900 million viewers tuning in weekly which is higher than the audience reached by any radio show ever!
